üöÄ Quick Start Guide

  • Step 1: Sort cards by sport BEFORE using this system
  • Step 2: Separate graded cards from raw cards
  • Step 3: Group obvious lots (5+ of same player/team)
  • Step 4: Fill out the intake form for each group
  • Step 5: Apply matching physical sticker (red/yellow/green)

üì¶ Shipping Rules (Applied at Listing)

  • Under $20 single raw card: eBay Standard Envelope ($0.69 cost, charge $1.25-1.99)
  • Any graded card: USPS Ground minimum ($4.99)
  • Lot of 5+ cards: USPS Ground minimum ($4.99)
  • $20-99: USPS Ground ($4.99)
  • $100+: Premium shipping options available

üí∞ Profit Calculation

  • Formula: List Price - Cost Basis - (List Price √ó 13.25% eBay fee) - Shipping Cost
  • eBay Fee: Default 13.25% (editable per item)
  • Cost Basis: What you paid for the cards
  • Shipping Cost: Your actual shipping expense

üíæ Data Management

  • Auto-Save: All entries are saved to your browser automatically
  • Export: Download your inventory as CSV for backup or Excel use
  • Import: Upload a CSV to restore or bulk add items
  • Clear: Remove all data (cannot be undone!)

üì¶ Box Break Calculator

  • Create Box: Go to Box Break tab and enter box name, cost, and expected cards
  • Allocation Methods:
    • Equal Distribution: Divides cost equally (e.g., $120 box √∑ 36 cards = $3.33/card)
    • Value-Weighted: Allocate based on market value (manual entry)
    • Zero-Cost Commons: Assigns $0 to commons, full cost to hits
  • Active Box: Only one box can be active at a time. Cost basis auto-fills when adding cards
  • Auto-Complete: Box deactivates automatically when expected cards reached
  • Tracking: Each card remembers which box it came from
